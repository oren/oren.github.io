# feelings

Are you angry?
Nǐ shēngqì ma
你生气吗?

我没有啊
为什么这么问？

Wǒ méiyǒu a.
Wèishéme zhème wèn?

I am not.
Why do you ask?

我很好奇
Wǒ hěn hàoqí
I was curious

---

To Feel = gǎn shòu dào / 感受到

---

Examples:

I can **feel** your love

我能**感受到**你的爱

Wǒ néng **gǎn shòu dào** nǐ de ài
