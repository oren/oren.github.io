What Should You Wear?

What you wear depeds on the water temperature. For example:

| Temperature (Fahrenheit/Celsius) | Wear | | --- | --- | | 46-53/8-12 | 5/4mm Hooded Fullsuit, 5mm Boot, 5mm Glove | | 56-64/13-18 | 3/2mm Fullsuit | | 74+/23+ | UV (Rashguard), Boardshorts |
