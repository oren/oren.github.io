Nonviolent Communication (NVC)

Nonviolent Communication (NVC) is a communication process developed by Marshall B. Rosenberg, a psychologist and mediator, that focuses on creating compassionate connections between people. NVC is designed to help individuals express their needs and feelings effectively, listen empathetically to others, and resolve conflicts peacefully.
