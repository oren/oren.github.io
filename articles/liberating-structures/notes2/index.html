Liberating Structures Meetup 2020-02-22

We used the following structures:

  1. 5 by 5 Writing - answer 5 questions about relationships. This will be input for the next structure
  2. Ecocycle Planning - visualize your relationships on a maturity scale
  3. Helping Heuristics - visualize the approach your friend help you (Quiet Presence, Guided Discovery, Loving Provocation, Process Mindfulness)
  4. Appreciative interviews - provide example of someone that helped you. Which approach did they use?
