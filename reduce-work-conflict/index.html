Reduce conflicts at work

Prepare a script for a difficult conversation at work.

  1. Think of a recent conflict with a co-worker. Enter their name.
  2. What did they say or do?
  3. How did you feel?
  4. What do you need from them so you will feel better?
  5. I am hearing that you want to feel [emotion] because [un-met need]. Is this accurate?
  6. Would you like to have a conversation with them to make them aware of how you feel and what you need?
  7. Here is my suggestion for your conversation with them: [observation], [feeling], [unmet need], [request]. Does this help you to be able to have a conversation with them?
