Chili Soup

Ingredients: * olive oil, onion, a few garlic cloves * sweet potato, a few bel peppers, 2 type of beans * Beef/pork/chicken (optional) * 4 cups of vegetable broth, crushed tomatoes * salt, chili powder, chipotle chili powder, cumin

How to make it: * In instant pot, saute olive oil, onion, and garlic. * Add everything else, seal the pot, and cook for 7 minutes
