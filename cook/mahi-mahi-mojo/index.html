Grilled Mahi Mahi with mojo

Ingredients:

Mahi mahi, olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, 1⁄4 cup lime juice, cilantro, green leaves (Arugula, spinach, etc)

How to make it:

Grill the fish (or in skillet): * mahi, olive oil, salt, pepper * 5 min each side grill

Make the sauce: * add to a bowl: lime juice, salt, cilantro * add to a pot: olive oil, garlic, salt. Put on stove for a few minutes until golden * add hot pot into the bowl

Serve: * serve the mahi on top green leaves and drizzel the hot sauce
