# Shrimp Avocado Salad

![shrimp](shrimp.jpeg)
![shrimp](shrimp2.jpeg)

**Summary**

Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 0 mins | Total time: 5 mins | Servings: 4

**Ingredients:**

- 12 oz cooked shrimp
- 2 large ripe avocados, cubed
- 1 cup corn
- 2 tbsp greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
- 1 tbsp sriracha
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**

1. Cut avocados in half and remove pit. Scoop flesh into a bowl and cube.
2. Add shrimp, corn, greek yogurt, lime juice, sriracha, salt, and pepper.
3. Toss until well combined and serve.
