## Tofu

![](tofu.png)
![](tofu2.jpeg)

**Ingredients:**

* Tofu, olive oil, sesame oil, soy sauce, garlic powder, paprika 

**How to make it:**

Put in air fryier for 10-15 min. Flip in the middle. I used a Ninja but you can also steam, bake, or pan fry it.
