Daikon Salad

Ingredients:

  • Daikon (white radish)
  • Sesame seeds
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice Vineger
  • Sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Bonito flake(fish flakes)
  • Shredded nori seaweed

How to make it:

  1. Put everything in a bowl.
  2. Cut daikon - pile the skin and cut into thin sticks.
  3. Add daikon to bowl of dressing and mix.
  4. Serve with bonito flakes and nori.
