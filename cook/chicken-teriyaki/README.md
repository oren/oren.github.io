# Chicken Teriyaki

Prep time: 5 min
Cooking time: 45 min

**Ingredients:**

* 1 tbps soy sauce
* 1/4 tbsp honey
* 1/4 tbsp sugar
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/2 tsp ginger
* 1/4 cup sake or wine

**How to make it:**

* 45 minutes in oven. 350F
* Flip over in the middle
