Garlic Noodles Recipe

1 lb. of noodles (Asian, spaghetti, etc.)

3-4 Tbsp. olive oil
3 Tbsp. minced garlic (5-6 cloves)

1 Tbsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. oyster sauce
1 Tbsp. fish sauce
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
2 Tbsp. water
1 Tbsp. soy sauce (Low Sodium)

In large pan, heat oil, reduced heat and add garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add garlic powder, oyster sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, water & soy sauce.

Boil noodles in water in large pot (don't overcooked - it will cook fast since it is fresh).

Drain and pour noodles into pan with sauce and toss until coated. 

Optional: Add parmesan cheese and mix. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve warm.  Toss in cooked diced lobster, shrimp or chicken or Scallop
