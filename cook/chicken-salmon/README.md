**Ingredients:**
* Salmon, chicken, eggs, shirataki noodels, omeboshi, tobiko (fish roe) cabbage, salt, pepper soy sauce, seasame oil, rice vineger, bonito, seasame seeds

**How to make it:**
* Cook with a pan. Serve with cold shirataki and tobiko
