Lentils and butternut squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of yellow lentils
  • Butternut squash
  • Yellow onion, diced
  • A few celery stalks
  • A few garlic cloves

Spices:

  • Paprikah
  • Cumin
  • Salt

How to make it:

  1. Chop everything
  2. In a pot, satee the onion for a few minutes
  3. Add the spices
  4. Add the garlic and satee for a few minutes
  5. Add the cerery and the lentils
  6. Cover everything with water
  7. When boil, lower the heat. Total cooking time around 30 minutes
