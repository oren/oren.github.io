Salmon Avocado Salad

3 servings

Salad * 4 cups mixed greens (such as a spring mix) * l medium ripe avocados, pit removed and diced into 1-in cubes * 1⁄2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved * 1 medium cucumber, ends trimmed, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced into half-moons * 3 oz smoked salmon, thinly sliced * crumbled feta cheese

Dijon Honey Vinaigrette * 1⁄8 cup lemon juice * 1 tablespoons honey * 1⁄2 tablespoon Dijon mustard * 1⁄8 cup olive oil * 1 tablespoons minced fresh dill * Salt and pepper to taste * Pomegranate seeds and additional fresh dill for garnishing, optional
